This is me trying
to be a honeybee.

Francis Bacon talked about three insects.

The ant, who works hard, looks far, collects whatever tastes interesting and builds a heap of it. The spider, who is skillful and smart and spins elaborate, beautiful webs entirely by herself. And the bee — who gathers what the world has to offer, and transforms it into something sweet and useful for the world.

I have been an ant for a long, long time. I have sometimes been a spider.

This is Aastha trying to be a honeybee. Write to me if any of this tastes as sweet and useful to you as it did to me. Tell me if it tastes bitter.

cool, beautiful, borrowed light.
